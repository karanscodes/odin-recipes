Description

Homemade pizza is a simple and rewarding dish that begins with preparing a soft dough using flour, yeast, salt, and warm water, which is then kneaded until smooth and left to rise until doubled in size. Once ready, the dough is rolled out, topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and your choice of toppings, and baked in a preheated oven at 220°C (425°F) until the crust turns golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly, resulting in a delicious, freshly made pizza.

We will be making a classic recipe of Pizza. Crust full of Delicious and Tasty Cheese with Capsicum as the toppings for an incredible taste combo.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon yeast
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pizza or tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 small capsicum (bell pepper), sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, yeast, salt, warm water, and olive oil to form a soft dough.
  2. Knead the dough for about 8 to 10 minutes until smooth, then cover and let it rise for 1 hour.
  3. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  4. Roll out the dough into your desired pizza shape and thickness.
  5. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the base.
  6. Add shredded cheese and sliced capsicum on top.
  7. Sprinkle oregano and chili flakes if desired.
  8. Bake for 12–15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is melted.
  9. Remove, let it cool slightly, slice, and serve.
