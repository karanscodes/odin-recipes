Description

Homemade pizza is a simple and rewarding dish that begins with preparing a soft dough using flour, yeast, salt, and warm water, which is then kneaded until smooth and left to rise until doubled in size. Once ready, the dough is rolled out, topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and your choice of toppings, and baked in a preheated oven at 220°C (425°F) until the crust turns golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly, resulting in a delicious, freshly made pizza.
