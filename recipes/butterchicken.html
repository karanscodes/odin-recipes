Description

Butter chicken is a rich and creamy North Indian dish made with tender pieces of chicken cooked in a flavorful tomato-based gravy. The dish combines butter, cream, and aromatic spices to create a smooth and mildly spiced curry that pairs perfectly with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (boneless)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with spices and yogurt for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the chicken in a pan until lightly browned.
  3. In another pan, melt butter and add ginger-garlic paste.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until thick.
  5. Add spices and cooked chicken to the gravy.
  6. Pour in cream and simmer for 5–10 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with naan or rice.
